## Getting Started: Your 30-Day Plan

Here's a practical plan to kick-start your personal branding journey:

### Week 1: Foundation
1. Define your niche and unique value proposition
2. Audit your current online presence
3. Create or update your profiles on chosen platforms

### Week 2: Content Strategy
1. Develop your content pillars
2. Create a content calendar
3. Start batch-creating content

### Week 3: Engagement
1. Join relevant communities
2. Start engaging with industry leaders
3. Begin sharing your expertise

### Week 4: Analysis & Optimization
1. Review your analytics
2. Adjust your strategy based on data
3. Plan your next 30 days
